RISC-V and Linux

Open Source Benefits

The goal of this paper was to expand upon the benefits of open source technology, specifically concerning that of the RISC-V Instruction Set Architecture and the Linux Operating System. This paper focuses on a variety of topics, ranging from a comparison of Linux distributions to RISC-V based microcontrollers that can run Linux. This paper was co-authored with: Raj Anthony, Alex Torres, Adam Swartz, and Jason Yang.
